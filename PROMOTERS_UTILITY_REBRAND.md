# Promoters Page - Utility Framework Rebrand

## Overview
Successfully updated the Promoters page to align with the **Utility/Consumable** model, moving away from "Investment/ROI" language toward a **"Performance & Operations"** framework. This rebrand positions promoters as **"General Contractors of the Digital Stadium"** who earn **Service Fees** for **Successful Event Fulfillment**.

## Date Completed
May 1, 2026

---

## Key Strategic Changes

### 1. Core Positioning Shift
- **FROM**: "Promoter's Edge" / "Investment & ROI"
- **TO**: "Event Orchestrator" / "Service Fees & Operations"

### 2. Language Framework
| Old Term | New Term | Purpose |
|----------|----------|---------|
| Host Revenue | Event Service Fees | Payment for professional service |
| Earnings Breakdown | Service Fee Allocation | Clarifies compensation structure |
| The Promoter Advantage | The Orchestrator's Utility | Focus on tools & efficiency |
| Primary Ticket Sales | Access Pass Fulfillment | Service deliverable |
| Whale Revenue | Premium Experience Service | Fees for managing high-value services |
| Sponsorship & Ad-NFTs | Brand Inventory Management | Compensation for hosting assets |
| Instant Liquidity | Automated Settlement | Technical utility feature |
| Viral Amplification | Global Audience Engagement | Tool for network health |
| Ticket-to-App Funnel | Operational Pipeline | Service delivery process |
