# Fans Page Implementation

## Overview
The Fans page is the most critical conversion point for the 250 million users. It transforms the event from a "stream" into a **Livable Digital City**.

## Color Scheme
- **Electric Cyan (`#00F0FF`)**: Social actions, networking, connections
- **Electric Lime (`#C6FF00`)**: Earnings, rewards, economic opportunities

## Key Sections

### 1. Hero Section
- Bold headline: "Live the Event, Not Just Watch It"
- Subheadline about the Multi Billion Dollar Economy
- CTA: "Get Your Lifetime Ticket"

### 2. Fan Experience
- High-Profile Networking & Social Synergy
- Super Bowl Social Layer
- Real-World Integration (Food & Shopping)

### 3. Fan Economy
- Virtual Concierge opportunities
- Reputation Mining
- Affiliate Kiosks
- Node Support

### 4. Reputation Score System
- Bronze, Gold, and Legendary levels
- Visual progression indicator
- Perks breakdown

### 5. Success State CTA
- Lifetime Ticket acquisition flow (for early believers)
- Electron App download prompt

## Components Created
- `FanExperienceGrid.tsx` - Interactive feature showcase
- `FanEconomyTable.tsx` - Earning opportunities breakdown
- `ReputationLevels.tsx` - Tier system visualization
- `SocialIntegrationBadges.tsx` - Platform connection indicators
